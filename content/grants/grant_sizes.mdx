---
title: Grant Sizes 
description: Description of grant sizes that can be requested from the Iron Fish Foundation
---
Grants can be small (\$500 USD) or very very large (\$50k+) depending on the team, scope of the project, and projected maintenance cost.

| Grant Types | Payment Range (in USD or crypto equivalent) | Estimated Time Range | Estimated Workload/Team                                                 | Examples of Projects                                                                     |
| ----------- | ------------------- | -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Small       | \$500 - \$5k        | 1 - 3 months         | • 1 - 2 Developers<br />• 0 - 1 Designers                               | • Hashing Upgrades/Updates<br />• Maintenance<br />• Server Cost Subsidization           |
| Medium      | \$5k - \$50k        | 4 - 5 months         | • 2 - 3 Developers<br />• 0 - 2 Product Managers<br />• 1 - 2 Designers | • Mining Pool Integrations<br />• Publicly Accessible RPC Endpoints<br />• NFT Launchpad |
| Large       | \$50k+              | 6+ months            | • 4+ Developers<br />• 2+ Product Managers<br />• 2+ Designers          | • Cross Chain Bridge<br />• Cross Chain Protocols<br />• NFT Browser Extension           |

This table is intended to be a used as a guide. Please apply for the grant you think is most applicable to your project. If we think your idea is viable, we’ll reach out via Telegram for further discussion.
