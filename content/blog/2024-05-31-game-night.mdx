## Hello everyone!

One of our favorite moments of the Iron Fish Ambassador program that recently ended was getting to hang out with all of you 🥰 during trivia — so we de decided to expand that and today we're launching an entire Game Night program.

Come join us for poker, chess, trivia and many other games to hang out with fellow Iron Fishers and win cool swag 😎.

We'll be hosting a game *every day of the week* (Monday - Friday) to cover many timezones and variety of games. 

### How to Participate

🦈 Join [#fishy-chat](https://discord.gg/fbgwBb3mhn)  
👾 Show up and play  
🥇 Win Games & Earn Points  
🔝 [Track #fishy-points](https://discord.gg/9MYcY5DjYN)  
🦀 [Redeem Points](https://discord.gg/sRSAt7RP3z)  
😎 Get cool Swag!   

### When and Where to Play

Game “Nights” will be announced on Discord in our Events channel and will be played in the [game-chat](https://discord.gg/fbgwBb3mhn). They can also be found on our  [Community Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/1?cid=YWRtaW5AaXJvbmZpc2gubmV0d29yaw). This program will begin on Friday May 31st 2024, with games starting on Monday June 3rd 2024 and running through Friday October 4th at 5 pm PT.

### Who Can Play, Win, and Redeem Points

*Anyone over the age of 18 is currently eligible to participate in any hosted game and win*! (Please make sure to follow our rules and  [be excellent to each other](https://discord.gg/G9TgbuMVD9)!)

Eligibility requirements for point redemption are a little more complicated, so please see our [official rules](https://www.notion.so/Game-Night-Official-Rules-99b1aea61ebc4e3fa698fbe650bb17bb?pvs=21) to see if you’re eligible to redeem points for swag.
